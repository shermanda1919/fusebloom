Our Mission

You have big dreams. But as a high schooler, it’s tough to find internships where you can use your skills to change the world. We know how you feel searching for hours on end for programs, so we’ve designed a platform that will enable you to experience next level opportunities from next level people.

When our companies are looking for interns, they’re looking for hardworking, driven people like you who will be able to persevere past problems. Signing up for FuseBloom gives you access to a wealth of internships. All custom curated to your skills, location, and interests.

For companies who are searching for high school interns, we have a network of bright, talented students from around the world with skills ranging from front end web development to social media marketing. We understand that finding qualified interns is tough, so we’re connecting the next generation of innovators to next generation companies like you.
